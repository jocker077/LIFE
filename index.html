LIFE

Some say life is great
But others treat life with hate
Nothing to do as much
Nothing to think as such
Suicide on the left and right
Their loved ones filled with shock and fright



Depression getting strong
Sad emotions getting long
Happiness fading away
But all to say it is too late...



Life isn't a person and is never easy
So treat it like it's a baby
Nothing in it might be fun
But don't let your story be quickly done
